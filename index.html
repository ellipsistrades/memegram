How to read this
  • Follower @15m/1h/6hThe median outcome for someone who bought the moment the call was posted and held.
  • Hit rateShare of calls that at least briefly reached +30% within 15 minutes — what the channel will screenshot.
  • Dump rateShare of calls down 30%+ within the hour — what they won't screenshot.
  • Insider %Share of calls where the token was already up 25%+ in the 3 minutes before the public post — someone was buying before you were told.
  • ProvisionalChannels with 20–29 tracked calls: shown but flagged — early data, softened verdicts, and kept out of our public receipts posts. They reach full ranking only at 30+ calls.
Method. A bot sits in each channel and records every posted contract address with a timestamp. "Buy at the call" uses the first real market price after the post (never before — no hindsight). Price via Jupiter/DexScreener every 10s for 30 min, then every 60s for 6h. Pre-post run-up via 1-minute OHLCV candles. The pre-post figure quoted in the hero is the median run-up across every tracked call (how far the median call had already moved in the 3 minutes before its public post). The best-call showcase always shows its 1-hour follower outcome alongside the peak — we never quote a peak without the receipt. Nothing here is financial advice; it's a record of what happened.
